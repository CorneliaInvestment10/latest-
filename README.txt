Where to put the NBC trackable link:
Open config.js and replace:
https://REPLACE-WITH-YOUR-NBC-TRACKABLE-LINK.com
with your NBC partner tracking link.

Lead capture:
The form saves leads to browser local storage and lets you export a CSV from leads.html. This is useful for testing and simple local exporting. For live website lead storage across all visitors, connect the form to a true lead database such as Google Sheets, Airtable, HubSpot, Zoho, Mailchimp, or a Vercel database.

Important:
Do not delete the assets folder. It contains the locked preview images used for the exact page appearance.
